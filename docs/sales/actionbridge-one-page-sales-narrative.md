# ActionBridge — One-page sales narrative

## Positioning

ActionBridge is the Universal Agent Connector / Customer Setup Capability OS for Schwarzwald-Agent.

Schwarzwald-Agent thinks and talks. ActionBridge connects, translates, controls, approves, audits, and can kill-switch execution.

## Customer promise

You do not need to bring a perfect API. Verify your approved website, install one lightweight bridge where needed, choose what the agent may do, and keep approval/audit between AI and your business systems.

## Demo path

1. Operator creates setup link.
2. Customer verifies domain.
3. Customer installs connected-only bridge.
4. Customer enables read/draft capabilities.
5. Schwarzwald-Agent receives safe tools.
6. Draft/write actions stop for approval.
7. Audit timeline proves what happened.

## Safety story

- HTTPS-only target normalization.
- Digest-only setup tokens.
- Exact Origin bridge handshake.
- Server-owned allowlists.
- No destructive/transactional tools in MVP.
- Redacted audit events.
- Kill switch stays visible.

## MVP price logic

ActionBridge sells the hard part: turning messy customer websites/apps/backends into safe agent capabilities without custom one-off integrations for every customer.
